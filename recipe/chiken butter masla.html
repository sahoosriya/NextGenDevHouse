chiken butter masla

Description :-

  • Chicken Butter Masala is a popular Indian dish of grilled chicken simmered in a smooth,
    silky and creamy onion tomato and cashew gravy. This finger-licking good curried dish is popular on the restaurant menus.
    Also known as Butter Chicken Masala, this is a modified version of the authentic North Indian Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani).

Ingredients :-

> 4 Tbsp oil
> 100 gms butter
> 2 tsp ginger garlic paste
> 1/2 kg chicken (boneless cubes)
> 300 gms tomatoes (blended)
> 1 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
> 1 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
> 2 tsp curry powderto taste salt
> 4 tbsp kasoori methi
> 300 gms fresh cream
> 1 ginger, julienne
> For garnish:
> Green chillies
> 2 tbsp green coriander, chopped

Instructions :-

1.Heat oil in a wok.
2.Add ginger-garlic paste and chicken.
3.Fry till chicken changes colour to golden brown.
4.Add butter to chicken in the pan and then add blended tomatoes.Chicken Butter Masala.
5.Stir on heat for 2-3 minutes.
6.Add red chilli powder, turmeric powder, curry powder, salt and stir lightly till oil separates. Add kasturi methi.
7. Slowly add cream. Add ginger juliennes and cover with lid for few minutes.Chicken Butter Masala.
8.Garnish with green chillies, green coriander and serve with boiled rice.
