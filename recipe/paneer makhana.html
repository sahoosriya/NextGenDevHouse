Paneer makhani

Description :-

  • Paneer makhani is a delicious curry made by cooking paneer in buttery tomato cashew gravy. This super delicious creamy paneer makhani is loved by most Indians & is popular on the Indian restaurant menus. Paneer makhani goes so well with plain basmati rice, jeera rice, roti, paratha or naan.

Ingredients of Paneer makhani :-

1.1 onion, sliced
2.3 tomato, sliced
3.2 tbsp cashew
4. bay leaf
5.3 pod cardamom
6.3 cloves
7.1 inch cinnamon
8.1 tsp ginger garlic paste
9.1 tsp butter
10.2 cup water
11.2 tbsp butter
12.2 tbsp oil
13.15 cubes paneer
14.1 tsp cumin
15.1 chilli, slit
16.1 inch ginger, chopped
17.¼ tsp turmeric
18.1 tsp chilli powder
19.½ tsp coriander powder
20.¼ tsp garam masala
21.2 tbsp cream
22.¾ tsp salt
23.1 tsp kasuri methi, crushed
24.2 tbsp coriander, finely chopped

Steps :-


Step 1:-
firstly, to prepa re onion tomato base, in a pan take 1 onion, 3 tomato and 2 tbsp cashew.

Step 2:-
add 1 bay leaf, 3 pods cardamom, 3 cloves, 1 inch cinnamon and 1 tsp ginger garlic paste. also, add 1 tsp butter and 2 cup water.

Step 3:-
mix well, cover and boil for 10 minutes or until everything are cooked well then remove the bay leaf and cool completely before grinding to smooth paste. keep aside>

Step 4:- in a large kadai heat 2 tbsp butter, 2 tbsp oil and fry 15 cubes paneer. once the paneer turns light golden, keep aside.
step 5:-
in the same oil, add 1 tsp cumin, 1 chilli and 1 inch ginger. saute on low flame until the spices turn aromatic. keeping the flame on low, add ¼ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chilli powder, ½ tsp coriander powder and ¼ tsp garam masala.

step 6:-
saute on low flame until the spices turn aromatic. pour in a prepared onion tomato base and mix well. keep mixing until the oil separates.

step 7:-
also, add 2 tbsp cream and ¾ tsp salt. mix well. cover and boil for 5 minutes or until the base is cooked well.

step 8:-
add in fried paneer, 1 tsp kasuri methi and 2 tbsp coriander. mix well. finally. enjoy paneer makhani with roti or rice.
